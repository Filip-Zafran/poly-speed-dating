Venue Manager

CRITICAL URGENT

In charge of everything venue-related

As our Venue Manager, you'll be the driving force overseeing all aspects of our physical space for PolyFest 2026. You'll scout and secure the ideal venue across Berlin, negotiate contracts, manage all logistics including capacity planning, accessibility requirements, and technical needs (sound, lighting, WiFi, furniture, catering). You'll oversee setup and breakdown, coordinate with all teams on venue needs, ensure the space embodies our values of inclusivity and community, and manage the day-of venue operations. This role is critical to the event's success and requires strong leadership, communication skills, negotiation experience, and a deep understanding of what makes a space welcoming for the polyamorous community.

Workshops

Curate educational and engaging experiences

As the Workshops coordinator, you'll be responsible for curating and organizing engaging educational experiences for PolyFest 2026. You'll work with facilitators and presenters to develop workshops that are inclusive, informative, and aligned with our community values. From initial concept development through execution, you'll manage scheduling, logistics, material preparation, and ensure every workshop creates a welcoming and safe learning environment.

This role requires creativity, organizational skills, excellent communication abilities, and a passion for community education. You should have experience coordinating events or educational programs and understand the needs of diverse learners in our polyamorous community.

Design

Shape the visual identity of PolyFest

As our Design lead, you'll be responsible for shaping the visual identity of PolyFest 2026. You'll work on branding, graphic design, web design, and overall aesthetic direction to ensure consistency across all touchpoints. From the main event materials to promotional content, you'll create designs that reflect our community values and make PolyFest visually distinctive and memorable.

This role requires strong design skills, knowledge of current design trends, and the ability to work collaboratively with other teams. You should be comfortable with design tools and software, and have experience creating cohesive visual systems.

Sponsors

Build partnerships and secure support

As our Sponsorship lead, you'll be responsible for identifying potential sponsors, developing partnership proposals, and building mutually beneficial relationships with organizations aligned with PolyFest values. You'll manage sponsor agreements, coordinate sponsorship activation, and ensure sponsors receive value from their investment.

This role requires strong networking skills, business acumen, excellent communication abilities, and experience in business development or sponsorship management. You should be comfortable with sales processes and have a genuine passion for supporting our community event.

Volunteers Coordinator

Build and support our volunteer community

As the Volunteers Coordinator, you'll be responsible for recruiting, organizing, and supporting our volunteer community for PolyFest 2026. You'll handle volunteer applications, create role descriptions, organize training, schedule shifts, and ensure volunteers feel appreciated and supported throughout the event. You'll also manage volunteer logistics and communication.

This role requires strong organizational skills, excellent people management abilities, enthusiasm for community engagement, and experience working with volunteers. You should be able to inspire and motivate people, resolve conflicts, and create a positive volunteer experience.

Social Media Manager

Build our online community presence

As our Social Media Manager, you'll be responsible for building and maintaining PolyFest's online community presence across all social platforms. You'll create engaging content, manage community interactions, coordinate social campaigns, and track analytics to measure impact. You'll be the voice of PolyFest in the digital space.

This role requires excellent written communication skills, creativity, understanding of social media algorithms and trends, and experience managing social platforms. You should be able to create engaging content, build community, respond to feedback, and work with other teams to amplify their messages.

Disclaimer: These are rough role descriptions that would need to be filled. Each position can be discussed and adapted to match the skills, capacities, and interests of the applicants. We believe in flexible, collaborative teamwork and are open to shaping roles that work best for PolyFest and our team members.
