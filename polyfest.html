What you want to do

Venue Manager

CRITICAL
  • Scout and secure the ideal venue across Berlin
  • Manage logistics: capacity, accessibility, technical needs (sound, lighting, WiFi)
  • Oversee setup, breakdown, and day-of operations
  • Ensure the space reflects our values of inclusivity and community

Design & Social Media Manager

  • Shape visual identity: branding, graphics, promotional materials
  • Create engaging content for social platforms (Instagram, etc.)
  • Build and manage online community presence
  • Ensure consistency across all design and digital touchpoints

Marketing & Sponsors

  • Identify and build partnerships with aligned sponsors
  • Develop sponsorship proposals and agreements
  • Coordinate marketing campaigns and brand awareness
  • Ensure sponsors receive value from their investment

Volunteers Coordinator

  • Recruit and organize volunteer community
  • Handle applications, training, and shift scheduling
  • Ensure volunteers feel appreciated and supported
  • Manage volunteer logistics and communication

Communication

  • Manage internal team communication and coordination
  • Handle external messaging to community and attendees
  • Create and distribute newsletters and updates
  • Ensure consistent messaging across all channels

Other: New Ideas / What You Want to Do

  • Do you have a skill or passion that could help PolyFest?
  • Interested in creating a new role or combination of roles?
  • Let us know your ideas and how you'd like to contribute!
  • We're open to flexible, collaborative teamwork

⏰ Deadline to Apply: 31 July 2026

How to Apply?

We'd love to hear from you! Apply for any role by sending us:

  • A few short motivation sentences — Tell us why you're interested and what excites you about PolyFest
  • Your CV (optional) — Help us understand your background and experience
  • Your LinkedIn profile (optional) — Or any other way you'd like to share your work

Send everything to: polyspeeddating@gmail.com

We'll review applications and get back to you with next steps. Questions? Reach out anytime!

Note: These are rough role descriptions that would need to be filled. Each position can be discussed and adapted to match the skills, capacities, and interests of the applicants. We believe in flexible, collaborative teamwork and are open to shaping roles that work best for PolyFest and our team members.
